Time of Refreshing

God, You made us male and female; and thus we leave father and mother, and cleave unto our partners; and we two shall be one flesh. What You, God, have joined together, let no man put asunder. Because of the hardness of our hearts, You have allowed us to divorce, 'though from the beginning it was not so. Whosoever puts away their spouse, except it be for fornication, and marries another, commits adultery, as well as those whom they marry. Grounded in this partnership, we transcend our individual power, and walk together into Your Lightness. You are not about you versus me. Your are not about us versus them. You are about all of us being together. Alone we did and do fail You. We killed the Prince of Life. I kill the Prince of Life. You raised Him from the dead. We can approach You only together. Faith in His name makes us strong. Our faith makes us strong. What I have done in ignorance -- of that I do now repent, so that our sins might be blotted out, when Our time of refreshing comes in the presence of the Lord.
